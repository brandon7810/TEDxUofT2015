As a tri-campus initiative, TEDxUofT aims to showcase talent from the University of Toronto. If you have come across fascinating work, stories, or ideas of individuals from UofT, whether they are faculty, staff, students, or alumni, please don’t hesitate to nominate them to speak at the TEDxUofT2015 conference! This is a great opportunity for their ideas and experiences to be recognized by a larger audience. If you have other speaker suggestions, we also accept nominations of those who are external to UofT.
